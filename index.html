Welcome to Virginia Beach

Virginia Beach is located on the Atlantic Ocean at the mouth of the Chesapeake Bay. It is also the most populous city in Virginia, with approximately 450,000 residents. Not only does it provide a place to live and raise a family, but it also has many tourist attractions that offer visitors plenty to do.
